What is Internet of Thing?

The Internet of Things (IoT) refers to the interconnected system of physical devices, vehicles, buildings, and other objects embedded with sensors, software, and network connectivity, which enables these objects to collect and exchange data. The goal of IoT is to create smart environments that improve efficiency, safety, and convenience by allowing objects to communicate and interact with each other and with human users.

Internet of Things

The Internet of Things (IoT) represents a significant technological advancement where everyday objects are connected to the internet, enabling them to send and receive data. This interconnected system involves various devices, ranging from household items to industrial machinery, all embedded with sensors, software, and network connectivity. The primary aim of IoT is to create smart environments that enhance efficiency, safety, and convenience through automation and real-time data exchange.
