What is Network Security?

Network security refers to the strategies and technologies used to protect the integrity, confidentiality, and availability of an organization's network and data. It encompasses a wide range of practices and measures designed to prevent unauthorized access, misuse, malfunction, modification, destruction, or improper disclosure of network resources.
